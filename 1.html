FACTS & KITCHEN

Fun Fact

The word panini is Italian for “small breads.” English made it singular. Ottawa made it lunch.

Kitchen Notebook: 5‑Minute Tomato Garlic Soup

Simmer equal parts crushed tomato and stock, stir in garlic, a pinch of sugar, salt, and pepper, then finish with a swirl of cream or butter. Excellent for panini dunking.
